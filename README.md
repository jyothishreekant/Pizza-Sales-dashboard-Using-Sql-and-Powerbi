# 🍕 Pizza Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The Pizza Sales Dashboard is an interactive Business Intelligence solution built using **Power BI** to analyze pizza sales performance. This dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights through interactive visualizations, helping businesses monitor revenue, customer purchasing behavior, and product performance.

The project demonstrates end-to-end data analytics skills, including data cleaning, SQL querying, data modeling, DAX calculations, and dashboard development.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to:

- Analyze overall business performance using key sales metrics.
- Identify sales trends across days and months.
- Evaluate the performance of pizza categories and sizes.
- Discover customer ordering patterns.
- Support data-driven business decisions through interactive reporting.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Friday records the highest number of customer orders.
- August is the best-performing month based on total orders.
- The Classic Pizza category generates the highest revenue and sales volume.
- Large-size pizzas contribute nearly 46% of total revenue.
- Customers purchase an average of 2.32 pizzas per order.
- Weekend sales are significantly higher than weekday sales.
